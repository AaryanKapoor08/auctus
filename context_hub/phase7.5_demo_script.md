## üö® Common Demo Issues & Recovery Strategies

### **Issue: Page Takes Long to Load**
**Recovery:**
- Mention: "The AI is calculating personalized matches in real-time"
- Fill time by explaining what the page will show
- Check console for actual errors

### **Issue: Wrong Business Selected**
**Recovery:**
- Quick switch to correct business via navbar
- Say: "Let me switch to the right business profile..."
- Continue normally

### **Issue: Search/Filter Returns No Results**
**Recovery:**
- Immediately reset filters
- Say: "Let me adjust these filters to show more results"
- OR search for known term (e.g., "coffee")

### **Issue: Link Doesn't Work**
**Recovery:**
- Use navbar to navigate to intended page
- Say: "Let me navigate there directly..."
- Note issue for post-demo fix

### **Issue: Console Error Appears**
**Recovery:**
- Ignore if page still works
- If broken, refresh page
- Say: "Let me refresh to ensure we have the latest data"
- Continue from current section

### **Issue: Chatbot Doesn't Respond**
**Recovery:**
- Close and reopen chatbot
- Use quick action chips instead of typing
- If still broken, skip and move to next section
